About

Why this project exists

Most stock tools — Yahoo Finance, iOS Stocks — are built for investors who want to buy or sell shares. But many professionals use stock trends to understand the dynamics of a sector.

Existing tools make this difficult because they show only one company at a time and don't allow easy comparison across different timeframes.

StockView solves that by offering a simple way to view a portfolio of companies, compare their daily, weekly, and monthly performance, and spot trends at a glance — all on one clean dashboard.

Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks, no bloat. Just data, clearly presented.
